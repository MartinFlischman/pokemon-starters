What are Pokémon?

Pokémon are “monsters” that have special powers and share the world with humans. There are different types or categories of Pokémon: Bug, Fighting, Fire, Flying, Dragon, Electric, Ghost, Grass, Ground, Ice, Normal, Poison, Psychic, Rock, and Water. Within each type are several different Pokémon, each possessing various abilities.

They can evolve and pass through various levels, 100 being the highest. There are also special stones (Fire, Leaf, Moon, Thunder, and Water) that can be used to force some Pokémon to evolve into more powerful creatures. Of course, after normal development in skill learning, various Pokémon often evolve on their own.
